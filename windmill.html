Minerals and Energy Resources

Class 10 - Contemporary India II

Learning Objectives

  • Understand sources of power for electricity (renewable & non-renewable).
  • Explore principles and mechanisms of power generation.
  • Examine applications in domestic, industrial, and commercial sectors.
  • Analyze environmental and economic impacts for sustainable development.

Science Involved

Wind contains kinetic energy. Turbine blades rotate, producing mechanical energy. A generator converts it into electricity. Wind power is clean and renewable.

Participate & Observe

  • Observe rotation of windmill/turbine in moving air.
  • Notice faster wind increases blade speed.
  • Identify blades, tower, and generator.
  • Discuss conversion of wind energy to electricity without pollution.

Applications

  • Electricity Generation: Wind farms produce clean electricity.
  • Agriculture: Windmills provide mechanical power.
  • Water Pumping: Wind pumps lift water for irrigation.
  • Sustainable Farming: Supports eco-friendly practices.
  • Rural Development: Provides energy in remote areas.
